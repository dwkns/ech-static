The Breakfast Menu

As a trained and experienced chef, Penny is excited by food and changes the menus on a daily basis with new and innovative options based on the produce available.

We have both savoury and sweet daily specials such as Bubble and Squeak with Crisp Smoked Streaky Bacon, topped with a Poached Egg or Plum and Pecan waffles with lashings of maple syrup. Of course, there are some firm favourites which are available every day such as crunchy home-made muesli, with dried fruit, nuts and healthy grains. Our delicious fresh fruit smoothies, made with local seasonal fruits packed full of goodness.

And of course, a full English breakfast including the classics: Locally dry cured bacon, succulent sausages, home baked fried bread, oven roasted tomatoes, juicy Portobello mushrooms, black pudding, and free-range eggs, made any way you like, fresh from the neighbour’s chickens.

An equally delicious vegetarian Full English is also available, with Homemade Baked Beans, Griddled Avocado, Potatoes, Portobello Mushrooms, Oven Baked Tomatoes, and Eggs as you like them! We are happy to cater to your dietary requirements, ensuring that everyone can have a delicious breakfast! Please let us know of any dietary requirements at the time of booking so we can create something just for you.

Local, sustainably sourced food

We believe in buying the best but we also believe in buying locally; we buy very locally whereever and whenever possible. We use the village shop for fantastic produce including butter, cream and fresh vegetables such as asparagus and mushrooms and beautiful fish such as smoked trout & our neighbours in the village supply us with eggs and blueberries when in season. We grow all of our herbs in our lovely garden, as well as seasonal fruit such as rhubarb and gooseberries.

Our suppliers

The Ultimate Fresh Juices

Bloomberry of Devon

A small slightly mad company run by Chris and Sarah Jones nestled amongst the narrow lanes of Devon making amazing juices from natural ingredients and extract. Bloomsbury creates amazing and delicious award-winning juices by adding unique twists to classic juice combinations. Apple, Blueberry and Lavender juice is a favourite at Edgcott House.

Tea & Coffee

Miles of Minehead

Miles are a tea and coffee merchants based in West Somerset. Split between two sites, they taste, buy, blend and pack their own brand of tea, 'Miles Tea', in Minehead whilst their coffee is roasted, ground and packed in Porlock. A true family business with a passion for service and quality which is reflected in their award winning products which are served every day at Edgcott House

Our butcher

John May’s of South Molton

All meat sold by John May Family Butcher is butchered, processed and prepared on site by a team that has over 130 years butchery experience between them. The bacon used for Edgcott Houses breakfast is dry cured on the premises and smoked bacon is prepared in their own smoker on their farm. We also get our delicious sausages and black pudding from John Mays.

The best for all the rest

Somerset Local

Somerset Local's ethos is to make quality local food available to everyone in Somerset. Working with the finest farmers markets, they deliver the highest quality local produce from thier fammily of suppliers, directly to our door, every single day. It doesn't get fresher than that.
