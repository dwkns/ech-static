Edgcott House

Luxury Bed & Breakfast in the heart of
Exmoor National Park

Edgcott House is a historic country home situated in the heart of Exmoor National Park.

Nestled amongst beautiful Moorland, sweeping Oak-lined Valleys and the beautiful rugged coastline, Edgcott House is a luxurious bed and breakfast and is a perfect place to relax or use as your base to explore our beautiful surroundings.

Our rooms

We have taken great care to restore each of the 7 bedrooms at Edgcott house to the highest possible standards, with beautiful furnishings and modern appliances ensuring that you’ll have everything you need to enjoy an extra special stay with us.

The Breakfast

We really pride ourselves on the quality of our breakfast, which we prepare fresh every morning especially for you from the best seasonal, local produce available. Whether you want to indulge with dishes such as Banana Pancakes with Honey Yogurt and ginger syrup or keep it traditional with a hearty Full English Breakfast, our delicious daily changing breakfast menu is sure to satisfy.
